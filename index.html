ReadytoFly is a comprehensive digital program designed to help you overcome fear of flying using evidence-based cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and exposure therapy techniques. Our app guides you through personalized exercises, relaxation techniques, and gradual exposure to help you feel confident and comfortable when flying.
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a scientifically proven psychological treatment that helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. For flight anxiety, CBT helps you recognize irrational fears, challenge catastrophic thinking, and develop healthier coping strategies. It's been shown to have an 80-95% success rate for specific phobias.
Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) is a modern, evidence-based approach that helps you relate differently to anxious thoughts and sensations—rather than trying to eliminate them. It builds psychological flexibility through six skills: present-moment awareness, cognitive defusion (seeing thoughts as thoughts), acceptance, self-as-context, values, and committed action. For flight anxiety, ACT teaches you to notice “what-if” thoughts without getting hooked, anchor attention with simple grounding/breathing, clarify why flying matters to you (e.g., family, opportunities), and then take small, values-guided steps (graded exposures) even when discomfort shows up. ACT works well alongside CBT and exposure.
Unlike general relaxation apps or one-size-fits-all approaches, ReadytoFly uses graduated exposure therapy specifically designed for flight anxiety.
