ReadytoFly is a comprehensive digital program designed to help you overcome fear of flying using evidence-based cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) and exposure therapy techniques. Our app guides you through personalized exercises, relaxation techniques, and gradual exposure to help you feel confident and comfortable when flying.
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) is a scientifically proven psychological treatment that helps you identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. For flight anxiety, CBT helps you recognize irrational fears, challenge catastrophic thinking, and develop healthier coping strategies. It's been shown to have an 80-95% success rate for specific phobias.
Unlike general relaxation apps or one-size-fits-all approaches, ReadytoFly uses graduated exposure therapy specifically designed for flight anxiety. Our program adapts to your personal triggers and progresses at your pace. We combine multiple proven techniques including CBT, exposure therapy, and anxiety tracking to address both the psychological and physical aspects of your fear.
ReadytoFly is designed for adults of all ages who experience flight anxiety. Our users range from 18 to 80+ years old. The app is user-friendly with clear instructions and can be used by anyone comfortable with basic smartphone apps. Whether you're a digital native or new to apps, our step-by-step guidance makes it accessible for everyone.
